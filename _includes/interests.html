Interests

My personal interests include:

  • Machine Learning Pipelines. Creating models that are dynamically trained by data continually entering a system is an interesting problem not simply due to the mathematics involved, but also due to the architectural complexity required to handle such a workload and be reliable.
  • Computer Networks. Since my time working in the Simbiosys lab at Emory I have been interested in advances in technology that make it feasible to provide cheap, universal internet to developing countries.
  • Quantum Computing. Once we are able to circumvent the current limitations of fault tolerance and information retention time for quantum computers, the possibilities for modeling natural processes and data analysis will drastically increase.
