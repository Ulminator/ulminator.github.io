About Me

I have an inquisitive mind and am naturally drawn to taking on challenging problems. Previously I worked at SFL Scientific as a Data Science Consulting intern and The University of Cambridge as a VICHF-IREU fellow. I have enjoyed working with Node.js, React, Java, and Python and am open to positions with purely a backend focus or full-stack positions.
